Cookie Policy

Last updated: December 8, 2025

This Cookie Policy explains how this website (“the Site”) uses cookies and similar technologies to understand visitor activity and improve the Site’s performance.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember preferences, understand how users interact with pages, and improve user experience.

2. How This Site Uses Cookies

This Site uses cookies only through Google Analytics. These cookies do not identify you personally, but they help analyze how visitors use the Site.

2.1 Google Analytics Cookies

Google Analytics sets cookies to:

  • Distinguish unique visitors
  • Understand how visitors interact with the Site
  • Track pages visited, session duration, and interactions
  • Identify referring websites

All data collected is used solely to improve the Site’s design and performance.

For information on how Google uses this data, please visit:
https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

3. Types of Cookies Used

3.1 Analytics Cookies

These cookies collect information about how visitors interact with the Site. They do not store personal information such as names or email addresses.

3.2 No Marketing or Advertising Cookies

This Site does not use advertising cookies, remarketing cookies, tracking pixels, or third-party marketing technologies.

3.3 No Essential Cookies

Because this is a static portfolio website, no essential or login-related cookies are required.

4. Managing Cookies

Most browsers allow you to manage, disable, or delete cookies. Instructions can be found in your browser’s settings menu.

To opt out of Google Analytics tracking across all websites, you may install the browser add-on:

Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on
